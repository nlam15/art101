Watercolor painting

1. Clean up your work space
2. Get out your paints
3. Fill a cup with water halfway
4. Get a rag or paper napkin
5. Get out your paint brushes
6. Dip your brush into the water
7. Remove excess water from paint brush so that it is just damp
8. Swirl brush in desired color in paint palette
9. Paint and have fun
